# Resident Billing Lab 02 Subsidy Contract Update and Ledger Correction

Hands on lab simulating a subsidy contract update received after the billing period began, resulting in an incorrect subsidy payment and resident ledger balance.

## Objectives 

- Review subsidy contract data and effective dates
- Identify billing discrepencies caused by late contract updates
- Apply corrective adjustments while maintaining an audit trail

## Discrepency Identified

- April rent was charged correctly 
- Housing assistance payment of 600 was applied
- Resident payment of 250 was applied
- Updated subsidy contract indicates April subsidy should be 650
- Ledger reflects an incorrect remaining balance due to outdates subsidy amount
- Discrepency caused by late receipt of updated subsidy contract

# Resolution

- Reviewed updated subsidy contracy effective April 1, 2025
- Identified underapplied housing assistance payment
- Applied adjustment to reflect correct subsidy amount
- Recalculated resident ledger balance
- Confirmed accurate ending balance
- Maintained documentation for audit and compliance purposes
